# TA0027 - Initial Access (Tactic)

> The adversary is trying to get into your device.

## Goal
The adversary is trying to get into your device.

The initial access tactic represents the vectors adversaries use to gain an initial foothold onto a mobile device.

## Position in the attack flow
Phase 1 of the ATT&CK kill chain. An autonomous agent selects techniques from this tactic when its current sub-goal matches the objective above.
